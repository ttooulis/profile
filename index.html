Research Interests

My research interests generally fall in the field of Artificial Intelligence, especially in how AI and humans can work together. I'm focusing on areas like knowledge representation, reasoning, explainability, interpretability, and natural language understanding. My research aims to solve real-world problems, improving how people interact in natural language with AI systems. This includes helping people question AI decisions and ensuring these systems comply with regulations, norms and values.
